# P42-D003 gap-resolution evidence plan

## Status and scope

**PLANNED.** This document specifies future evidence needed to evaluate the proposed aggregate-governance and device/kernel-boundary traceability records. It does not implement a test, report a test result, approve either record, resolve either review gap, or approve P42-D003.

The identifiers D003-TRACE-AGG-001 and D003-TRACE-DEVICE-001 below are references to proposed P42-D003 traceability records documented elsewhere in this package. They are not CONF requirements, BUD resource budgets, P42-D governance decisions, implemented controls, or approved records. Both records remain **OPEN — RECOMMENDED** pending independent review and explicit project-owner approval. D003-GAP-AGG-001 and D003-GAP-DEVICE-001 remain **OPEN**.

Every future run must bind setup, control state, observations, artifacts, cleanup, and the terminal decision to the exact platform profile, attempt identity, authoritative process generation, policy versions, and evidence-ledger generation. Evidence must be produced by the trusted supervisor, durable owner, kernel or service control plane, and audit sink rather than by validator self-report. Numeric limits remain governed by later approved decisions and budgets.
