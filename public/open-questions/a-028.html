A-028 - SSI point-in-time basis versus annual flow basis

Open questions are published to keep the limits of the current record explicit.

Back to open questions Appendix A Open explorer

Why it matters

The SSI figure is computed from a published December 2021 basis, which is strong for that point in time but not a full annual transactional ledger.

Current web-edition status

Section 9 keeps the figure labeled on its Dec. 2021 basis and avoids overstating it as a harmonized annual program total.

Status: Open (figure carried at Medium, Dec-2021 basis)

Raised in: Section 1 Section 9

Record required to resolve it

A published annual SSI spending breakout for noncitizen recipients or a fully reconciled monthly series.
