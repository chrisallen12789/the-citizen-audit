A-025 - SNAP noncitizen participant share and benefit attribution

Open questions are published to keep the limits of the current record explicit.

Back to open questions Appendix A Open explorer

Why it matters

Participant composition is published, but a benefit-dollar breakout net of citizen children is not, and multiplying the participation share by the total would be prohibited modeling.

Current web-edition status

Section 8 publishes the participation mix and explicitly routes the missing dollar attribution to Section 13.

Status: Open -> Section 13

Raised in: Section 8

Record required to resolve it

A published SNAP spending breakout isolating noncitizen-attributable benefits net of citizen-child portions.
