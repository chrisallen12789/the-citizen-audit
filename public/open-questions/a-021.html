Open questions are published to keep the limits of the current record explicit.

Why it matters

Supplemental entrant populations can change ORR composition materially, but the public record does not fully isolate the amounts attributable to those parolee cohorts.

Current web-edition status

Section 6 keeps the parolee supplements visible as a data-gap item rather than allocating them heuristically.

Status: Open

Raised in: Section 6
