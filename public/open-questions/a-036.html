Open Question

A-036 - Whether Class C or D modeled estimates exist as bounded context only

Open questions are published to keep the limits of the current record explicit.

Back to open questions Appendix A Open explorer

Why it matters

Modeled or secondary estimates may exist for some gaps, but using them as adopted totals would violate the audit's evidence hierarchy unless they are clearly bounded as context only.

Current web-edition status

Section 13 leaves this open instead of backfilling opaque programs with secondary modeled estimates.

Status: Open

Raised in: Section 13

Record required to resolve it

A bounded inventory of Class C or D estimates that can be published as context without replacing missing primary evidence.
