Support

The research should stay public. Donations should fund the work without buying influence over the findings.

Donation system status

The donation page is now established. The payment processor still needs to be selected and connected. Until then, this page acts as the public funding policy and placeholder for donation buttons.

Contact about support Funding standard

What donations may fund

Hosting

Domain, Cloudflare, storage, and uptime costs.

Archives

Source capture, preservation, hash manifests, and link-rot protection.

Records

FOIA costs, public-record fees, document purchases, and source retrieval.

Research

Future audits, article production, data cleanup, and verification tools.

What donations do not buy

  • Donors do not get to approve findings.
  • Donors do not get to suppress findings.
  • Donors do not get private access to evidence that should be public.
  • Donors do not get to change the methodology.
  • Donors do not get to turn the site into partisan advocacy.

Premium access decision

The current recommendation is donations first, not a paywall. Public-interest evidence should remain public. If paid tools are ever added, they should be convenience features such as bulk exports, alerts, institutional dashboards, or API access — not access to the core audit record.
