Decision Log

The Decision Log is the audit’s reproducibility spine. It records methodology choices, source exclusions, definition rulings, data conflicts, scope limits, and gate decisions.

D-0001 through D-0043

Forty-three methodological rulings, ending in source-gate clearance.

Key public rulings

D-0001

Baseline trend window

The baseline window is FY2015 to present for budget and operations data, with longer baselines where official series begin earlier, such as suicide or homelessness.

D-0006

Toxic Exposures Fund treated as distinct stream

PACT Act and TEF trend lines must be shown both merged and unmerged, each labeled, because the funding structure changes the interpretation of VA growth.

D-0012

Suicide statistics must pair count and rate

Neither veteran-suicide count nor rate may be reported alone where the changing veteran population affects interpretation.

D-0022

Historical-growth passage quarantined

A CRS historical-growth passage contains an apparent internal inconsistency. The affected trend passage may not be cited until verified against the official PDF; unaffected table figures remain usable.

D-0032

Press-mediated and dual-basis rules

Figures or quotations that entered through press coverage carry visible flags. TEF decomposition must present act-provided and availability bases rather than single-basis headlines.

Source-gate refusal record

D-0039

Attestation was not enough

The author attested completion of the archive session, but the authoritative record still showed pending rows. The source gate stayed blocked because an assertion is not a record.

D-0040

Partial ingestion found defects

The completed manifest validated most captures, but duplicate-hash and canonical-URL defects prevented auto-promotion.

D-0041

Regression rejected

A corrected package would have erased validated archive status by replacing it with pending rows. The no-weakening rule rejected it.

D-0042

Artifact governed over attestation

A tag and dashboard narrative were treated as process evidence, not archive evidence. The file itself still carried defects.

D-0043

Source gate cleared

The resubmitted authoritative manifest validated cleanly: 42 archived, 1 terminal attempted failure, zero pending rows, zero duplicate hashes, zero duplicate canonicals, and zero malformed archived rows. Mechanical recommendation: ready with explicit limitations.
