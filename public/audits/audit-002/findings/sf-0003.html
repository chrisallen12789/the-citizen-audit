Audit 002 · Supported Finding

SF-0003

VHA staffing adequacy is measured by an instrument the agency publicly disputes.

All FindingsClaimsSource Gate

Finding statement

The public record supports a measurement-integrity finding. It does not support converting the shortage count into a complete measure of true staffing adequacy.

Claim basis

  • C-0011: FY2025 OIG severe occupational staffing shortage determination reported 4,434 severe shortages across VHA facilities.
  • C-0012: VA publicly disputed the usefulness of the mandated staffing-shortage instrument.

Source basis

Primary source ID: S-0025. The cited claim set is marked archived in the traceability register.

Reader boundary

This finding should be repeated as a measurement finding, not as a standalone conclusion about every VHA facility or occupation.
