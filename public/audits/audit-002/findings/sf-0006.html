Audit 002 · Supported Finding

SF-0006

Community care oversight records show growth beside measurement concerns.

All FindingsSource GateLimitations

Finding statement

Community-care records should be read by separating access expansion from the quality of the oversight measures used to evaluate that system.

Claim basis

  • C-0017: Appointment-timeliness standards and measurement limitations remain material to oversight conclusions.
  • C-0018: Community Care Network oversight includes unresolved network-adequacy and claims-data concerns.
  • C-0019: Community-care growth cannot be interpreted without separating access expansion from measurement adequacy.

Source basis

Primary source IDs include S-0111, S-0112, S-0116, and S-0105. The relevant source sets are marked archived in the traceability register.

Reader boundary

This finding is not a final community-care performance score. It identifies oversight and measurement boundaries that must travel with any claim about the program.
