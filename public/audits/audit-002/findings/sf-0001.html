Audit 002 · Supported Finding

SF-0001

The two 2024 VA funding episodes were structurally different events.

All FindingsClaimsSource GateLimitations

Finding statement

The public record supports separating the VBA and VHA 2024 funding episodes before discussing oversight, forecasting, or responsibility.

Claim basis

  • C-0006: VBA supplemental episode concerned benefits obligations and public reporting or communication.
  • C-0007: VHA supplemental episode concerned medical-care funding and forecasting under the Fiscal Responsibility Act cap.
  • C-0008: The approximately $5.1B carryover figure remains provenance-labeled and is treated as a limitation in v1.0.

Source basis

Primary source IDs: S-0105 and S-0106. The audit archive traceability register marks the cited source set for this finding as archived.

Reader boundary

This finding does not claim every internal cause is fully known. It claims the source record supports keeping the two episodes distinct.
