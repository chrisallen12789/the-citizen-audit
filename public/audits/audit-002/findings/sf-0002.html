Audit 002 · Supported Finding

SF-0002

FY2026’s record VA budget is primarily a mandatory-side event.

All FindingsClaimsEvidence MapLimitations

Finding statement

The FY2026 VA budget increase should be read through mandatory and discretionary components rather than as one blended increase.

Claim basis

  • C-0009: FY2026 enacted VA budget authority rose to $445.49B, with growth decomposing primarily through mandatory appropriations.
  • C-0010: Toxic Exposures Fund treatment is central to interpreting VA budget growth and requires basis caveats.

Source basis

Primary source ID: S-0007. The claim archive traceability register marks the cited source set as archived.

Reader boundary

This finding does not convert all VA budget growth into a program-performance conclusion. It is a budget-structure finding.
