EHR modernization remains a dated finding requiring currency checks.

All FindingsEvidence MapLimitations

Finding statement

EHR modernization findings in v1.0 are tied to dated oversight records and should be checked against newer records before reuse.

Claim basis

  • C-0016: EHR modernization findings are dated and currency-sensitive, including withhold deadlines and recommendation status.

Source basis

Primary source ID: S-0103. The cited source set is marked archived in the traceability register.

Reader boundary

This finding should not be treated as a permanently current EHR status page. It is a dated audit finding tied to the cited record.
