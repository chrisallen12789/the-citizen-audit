Audit 002 · Supported Finding

SF-0004

VHA-based suicide prevention is structurally bounded by reach.

All FindingsClaimsLimitations

Finding statement

VA’s reporting creates a boundary around spending-to-outcome claims because many veteran suicide decedents were outside VHA care in the prior year.

Claim basis

  • C-0013: VA’s 2025 suicide report indicates that 61% of veteran suicide decedents had no VHA health care in the prior year.
  • C-0014: Spending-to-outcome claims must partition VHA users and non-users because VHA reach is structurally incomplete.
  • C-0021: Suicide-report findings are bounded by reporting lag and population reach limitations.

Source basis

Primary source ID: S-0101. The cited source set is marked archived in the traceability register.

Reader boundary

This finding does not evaluate all prevention activity. It identifies a population-reach boundary that must be respected before making performance claims.
