14.10 Hostile-reader review

  1. Summing disbursements with obligations fails because Table 14-D uses single-type subtotals and shows the Section 3 obligations only as an alternate view.
  2. Blending fiscal years or merging the $50.9B cumulative into an annual figure fails because Lane 4 is never added to Lanes 1 or 2.
  3. Double-counting Section 3 military aid fails because D-017 keeps Section 5 net-new only.
  4. Treating ORR appropriations as spending fails because ORR sits only in the authority register.
  5. The federal-plus-state emergency Medicaid figure would have succeeded against a prior draft, but now the line is pending A-037 and carries no value in any subtotal.
  6. The SSI point-in-time basis partially lands as a survivable, disclosed limitation.
  7. The absence of a grand total does not land because a grand total across incompatible bases would be misleading.
  8. Total B indeterminacy is a disclosed central limitation, not a hidden defect.
  9. Section 13 gaps as “convenient omissions” do not land because each gap is registered with the owner, agency, and resolving evidence.
  10. Confidence laundering fails because confidence is weakest-link per lane with no cross-lane blend.
  11. Overseas dwarfing domestic is itself a finding, not padding.
  12. Section 3 including money to U.S. implementers lands only against Total B, not Total A, and that distinction is the point.

Verdict: Section 14 survives hostile review with disclosed limitations, not defects. No averaging, no blended total, no estimated gap, and no appropriation-as-spending remains in the locked section.

14.11 to 14.13 Lock checklist, reproducibility, and permanent flagged limitations

All 15 lock-checklist items pass: exclusions cite rules, no BA enters spending subtotals, no cross-lane sums exist, all Section 13 items remain in the gap register, Total A and Total B are never merged, confidence stays weakest-link, conflicting data are shown side by side, and every figure carries type, lane, date, Source ID, and confidence.

Second-researcher reproducibility attestation: an independent researcher with D-022, locked Sections 3, 5, 7, and 9, plus the Source Library, can reconstruct Lane 1 at approximately $71.9B, recurring obligations at approximately $1.6B per year, Lane 3 at approximately $2.21B, and Lane 4 at approximately $50.9B without judgment calls. Pending lines A-018 and A-037 carry no value and therefore do not affect any subtotal.

  • A-018 - ORR consolidated obligations / outlays not extractable; ORR appears in the authority register only.
  • A-037 - federal-only FY2023 emergency Medicaid share not isolated; the line carries no value.
  • A-005 - international-assistance U.S.-capture share unpublished, making Total B indeterminate for Section 3.
  • SSI basis - Lane 3 is point-in-time December 2021 at Medium confidence.

These do not block lock and remain permanent until superseded by v1.1+. Section 14 is locked in Version 1.0.
