Prototype Tool

Tax Dollar Explorer

Enter an amount and explore how the audit's currently measurable lanes compare. This is not a grand total. It is an interface for understanding scale while preserving the audit's basis-segregation rules.

Important Limitation

The explorer intentionally labels each lane by basis and source. It does not convert incompatible obligations, disbursements, cumulative figures, point-in-time computations, or federal-plus-state totals into one blended federal total.
