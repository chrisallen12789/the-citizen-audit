Press Room

For journalists, reviewers, and podcast hosts.

The Citizen Audit is built to be challenged. The preferred review method is to inspect the source chain, methodology, open questions, and correction process before discussing conclusions.

Short Description

The Citizen Audit is a living forensic audit of identifiable federal taxpayer spending directed toward non-U.S. recipients, with a documented map of where public federal reporting stops permitting measurement.

Suggested Framing

This is not a policy endorsement. It is an accounting and transparency project. Its principal finding is the boundary between what is publicly measurable and what is not.

Reviewer Request

Reviewers are asked to challenge figures by citing the section, table, Source ID, and replacement evidence. Correct challenges should become public corrections.
