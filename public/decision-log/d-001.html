D-001 - Gross, not net

Decision records preserve the publication's binding methodological rules in one addressable location.

Back to decision log Search related records

Published rule

Every adopted figure is gross money directed toward non-U.S. recipients or noncitizens. The audit does not offset those figures against taxes paid or any net-fiscal-impact model.
