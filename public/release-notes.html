Canonical release notes extracted from the publication PDF.

What this edition establishes. A known-minimum forensic accounting: overseas international assistance at approximately $71.9B disbursed in FY2023 and net-new military aid at approximately $50.9B cumulative plus approximately $1.6B per year recurring; domestic noncitizen SSI at approximately $2.21B and a published emergency-Medicaid figure of approximately $3.8B federal plus state in FY2023 with the federal share pending. All other domestic non-citizen spending is documented as unpublished in Section 13.

Form. Figures are presented as basis-segregated lane subtotals; no blended grand total is produced.

Permanent limitations. A-005, A-018, A-037, and the SSI point-in-time basis A-028. None affects any computed subtotal.

Known cosmetic items. Wide reference tables may wrap in print, and the Section 3 source file retains a v1.0 filename though its content is v1.1, corrected in the inventory.
